Victoria University uses Maths Wiz to help its undergraduates learn how to teach maths in primary school.

Maths Wiz and English Wiz are used by families in India, Australia, UK, Canada, USA, Malaysia, and New Zealand.

Maths Wiz and English Wiz includes over 30,000 questions, over 800 lessons, over 200 randomly generated tests, and over 70 games.

The current version of Maths Wiz and English Wiz are a result of a combined effort of over 175 man-years, since starting development in 1989.

Maths Wiz and English supports all the key areas of studies.

Maths Wiz and English Wiz programs are in over 1,000 schools.

Written with teachers and examiners.

Maths Doctor was developed with multiple tutors, teachers, and programmers. It took two years and many design changes until the assessment met our objective to accurately identify knowledge gaps across multiple years of the curriculum in less than one hour.
