School Partnership Program

Our Maths and English Wiz and Maths Doctor programs are available in over 1,000 schools across Australia. We understand that not all schools have the budgets, infrastructure, and/or resources to implement eLearning programs. This is why we developed our School Partnership program (otherwise known as the School Donation Program) to allow a limited number of schools the opportunity to have FREE access to our eLearning programs. Schools are also provided with free PD sessions for teachers so that teachers can have a greater appreciation of the programs and how to use this valuable resource.

The School Partnership Program, donation includes:

  • Donation of Maths and English Wiz for agreed year levels
  • Free access for ALL students during school hours (agreed year levels)
  • Limited number of Maths Doctor Licenses
  • Free technical support and assistance with set-up
  • Free PD sessions
